X-Wingman - X-Wing Miniatures Companion App

A small, lightweight and fast Companion App for the X-Wing Miniatures Game by FantasyFlight.

  • Manage your X-Wing Miniatures collection
  • Browse all available cards
  • Create and manage your squads
  • Print, save and share squads
