X-Wingman - X-Wing Miniatures Companion App

This is a simple and fast companion app for X-Wing Miniatures by Fantasy Flight Games. It is based on the great squad builder by Geordan Rosario.

Icons and fonts courtesy GeckoTH's excellent PSD, available on A Few Maneuvers.

X-Wing symbols font courtesy Hinny.

Fancy printout design inspired by TheKestrel on the FFG forums.

Maneuver dial functionality by hpanderson.
